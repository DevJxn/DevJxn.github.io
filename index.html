    Frequently asked questions

    Is this a one-time purchase or do I have to pay monthly for the clockfaces?

    All our clockfaces are a one-time purchase via k-pay. After you purchased the clockface, you don't have to pay a second time.

    I already paid for the clockface, why does it say that I have to pay another time?

    k-pay cannot recognise if you already purchased the clockface. So they think that you are a new customer. To unlock the clockface without paying a second time click here .

    I purchased a bundle. Do I get all clockfaces coming in the future too?

    This depends on which bundle you purchased. For example if your purchased the "All Clockfaces" bundle, you'll get all future clockfaces too. Otherwise you'll just get the clockfaces which will be added to the bundle, you purchased.

    I installed a clockface but there is no digit code. So i can't purchase it.

    Some products do have a free trial. After the trial ends it automatically shows you the digit code. However, if there is still no digit code after the trial, you can contact the k-pay support via: help@k-pay.io

    I can't remember which clockfaces I purchsased. How do I find them?

    You can find your purchase history here

    My question is not answered here. What do I do?

    For further questions you can check the k-pay FAQ here . Or contact us via E-Mail.
